How to Play Battle Royale
Step 1 of 5
1
Pick a Match & Game Mode
Browse live and upcoming matches from top leagues. Each card shows three game modes: ⚔️ Full Match (10 rounds, 90 min), ⚡ 2nd Half Sprint (7 rounds, starts at half-time), and 🔥 Final 15 (5 rounds, starts at 75th min). Modes unlock as the match progresses!
Tip
Short on time? Jump into a Final 15 — it's under 15 minutes of pure last-minute chaos. Want the full experience? Go Full Match for maximum rounds and glory.
2
Wait in the Lobby
Once you join, you'll land in the lobby. You can see who else is queued up — friends and strangers alike. The game starts automatically once enough players join or when the match kicks off. Get ready!
Tip
Share the match link with friends so you can battle each other in the same lobby!
3
Predict to Survive Each Round
The game runs 10 rounds of real-time predictions tied to what's happening in the match. Each round gives you a question like "Will Real Madrid equalise before halftime?" — pick your answer before the 15-second timer runs out. Correct? You survive. Wrong? You're eliminated.
Tip
Questions are dynamic — they adapt to what's actually happening in the match (goals, cards, corners), so watch the game and use your instinct!
4
Sudden Death — Last Players Standing
If 2 or more players survive all 10 rounds, the game enters Sudden Death. Rapid-fire questions with a shorter timer — the first wrong answer eliminates you. It's a head-to-head duel until only one champion remains.
Tip
Sudden Death questions are faster and trickier. Stay calm, trust your gut, and don't overthink!
5
Win & Climb the Rankings
The last player standing is crowned Battle Royale Champion! You'll earn ELO points based on your finishing position, which tracks your rank from Bronze all the way to Gold, Diamond, and Champion tiers. Collect match-specific badges and compete on the global leaderboard.
