What kind of league?

Pick the format that fits your crew. You can always adjust settings later.

Season-Long League Elite
Run a league across an entire competition — LaLiga, Premier League, Champions League. Every matchday adds to the standings all season.
Event League
Build a league around a specific event — NFL Draft, World Cup Final, a boxing match. Perfect for one-off spectacles.
Match Night
Quick league for a single match. Create it, share the link, play tonight. No commitment, pure fun.
Custom League
Set your own duration — a weekend tournament, a 2-week challenge, a month-long battle. You decide when it starts and ends.
