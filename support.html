Quick answers
The most common things people ask.
How do scoring multipliers work?
Points = base × time pressure × difficulty × streak × comeback × clutch. Live cards show estimated points before you answer.
Why was my question voided?
Usually because the match was cancelled, postponed, or the resolver couldn't read official stats. Voided questions don't affect your streak or score.
When does my Pro trial end?
Check your Game Center → Your Plan card. The "Resets in" timer counts daily caps; tier expiry is shown on the Profile page.
Can I delete my account?
Yes — message us via the form above and we'll handle the data deletion within 7 days.
